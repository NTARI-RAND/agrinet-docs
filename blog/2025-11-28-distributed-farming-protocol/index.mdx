      <span className={styles.briefingBadge}>Digitize Sunlight</span>

      <p className={styles.briefingText}>
        Planting plans, irrigation schedules, and harvest logs are digitized so sunlight-to-calorie conversion is visible,
        auditable, and optimizable for any neighborhood.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div className={styles.briefingCard}>
      <span className={styles.briefingBadge}>Edge Logistics</span>

      <p className={styles.briefingText}>

        labor changes. Waste drops while freshness rises.
      </p>
    </div>
